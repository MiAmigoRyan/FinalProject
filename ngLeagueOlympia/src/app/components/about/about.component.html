League Olympia Rules:


  1. League Creation:

    Any registered user can create a league in League Olympia.

    The league creator becomes the commissioner and has administrative control over the league.

  2. League Events:

    The commissioner selects a set of events for the league members to compete in.

    Events can be chosen from various sports, such as athletics, swimming, cycling, etc.

    Each event will have a predetermined list of available athletes to choose from.

  3. Joining the League:

    Other users can join the league by invitation or through public league listings.

    Once joined, users become players in the league.

  4. Athlete Selection:

    Players select an athlete from the list of available athletes for each event.

    Athlete selection is limited to one athlete per event.

  5. League Buy-In:

    Players have the option to buy into the league by contributing a predetermined amount of money.

    The buy-in creates a money pot that will be distributed among the winners.

  6. Side Wagers:

    Players can engage in side wagers with other league members on specific events and athletes.

    Side wagers are additional bets placed on top of the league buy-in and are settled separately between players.

  7. Scoring:

    The league is scored based on the athletes' finish results in each event.

    Athletes receive points based on their finishing positions: 1st place earns one point, 2nd place earns two points, and so on.

    The scores of the combined finishes for all the athletes picked by each player across all events are calculated.

  8. Determining the Winner:

    The winner of the league is the player with the lowest combined score from their athletes' finishes across all events.

    In the event of a tie, the tiebreaker can be determined by the commissioner or predefined rules.

  9. Prize Distribution:

    At the end of the league, the money pot is distributed among the winners based on the league's predetermined payout structure.

    The commissioner is responsible for ensuring fair distribution of the prize money.

  10. Fair Play and Conduct:

    All league members are expected to adhere to fair play and sportsmanship.

    Any form of cheating, collusion, or unsportsmanlike behavior may result in disqualification or removal from the league.

Note: These rules are subject to modification and customization by the league commissioner as per the requirements and preferences of the league.
